Our Reviews




Breakfast Club

French Toast

Review: If you don't mind waiting for a table or waiting for your food then I would recommend going to Breakfast Club. There is a reason it is so busy though and once your food arrives it is worth the wait!! I had crispy french toast the last time I was here. It is french toast covered in rice crispies with caramel on top and pan fried. Amazing!!! It's a little pricey but you won't even care once the food hits your mouth!



Taste of Mexico

Burrito

Review: Taste of Mexico more like Taste of Heaven, the quickest and friendliest service accompanies a plate of the best burrito with chips and salsa. Reservations are required however, its worth booking well in advance as seats are booked quickly! This resturant comes with an added bonus as it's not too pricey and very family friendly.








Italian Cafe

Pizza

Review: Upon exiting my car in the parking lot, I was immediately drawn to the scent of the fire oven. The atmosphere was modern, elegant and minimalistic which only adds to the general ambiance. I ordered the margarita pizza and was thoroughly impressed with the traditionally sweet sauce and bubbly crust. The portion size was perfect for dinner while the price was also reasonable.
